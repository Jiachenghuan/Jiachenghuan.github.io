## Record feeling before structure {#feeling-before-structure}
Your first reaction is often more valuable than your later analysis. Keep the feeling first, then return to structure.
## Three layers worth noticing {#three-layers}
### Language layer {#language-layer}
Notice the sentences that stop you, the words that cool a paragraph down, and the textures that change the pace of reading.
### Structure layer {#structure-layer}
Track how the text moves, where the rhythm slows, and why a turn in tone or point of view actually works.
### Personal layer {#personal-layer}
The reason the text hits you at this moment is not extra commentary. It is part of the reading event itself.
## Notes matter when they still answer back later {#notes-that-answer-back}
If you return weeks later and can still feel the question, the mood, and your own judgment, the note has become more than a quote bank.
